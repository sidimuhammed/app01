PEEPClinical▶
Positive End-Expiratory Pressure — pressure maintained in the lungs at the end of expiration in mechanically ventilated patients. Prevents alveolar collapse. Normal therapeutic range: 5–10 cmH₂O.
PPMDevice▶
Planned Preventive Maintenance — scheduled maintenance performed on medical devices to prevent failure, ensure accuracy, and maintain compliance with safety standards.
IBWClinical▶
Ideal Body Weight — used in ventilator management (tidal volume = 6–8 mL/kg IBW). Males: 50 + 0.91×(height cm − 152.4). Females: 45.5 + 0.91×(height cm − 152.4).
CMMSDevice▶
Computerised Maintenance Management System — software used to manage work orders, asset registers, PM schedules, and calibration records in a clinical engineering department.
SpO2Biomedical▶
Peripheral oxygen saturation measured by pulse oximetry. Normal range: 95–100%. Uses red (660nm) and infrared (940nm) light to differentiate oxyhaemoglobin from deoxyhaemoglobin.
IEC 62353Standard▶
Standard for repeated safety testing of medical electrical equipment after repair and during maintenance. Covers earth leakage, protective earth resistance, and patient leakage current.
